FishDet-M

A Unified Benchmark for Underwater Fish Detection with CLIP-Guided Model Selection

Lyes Saad Saoud, Muayad Abujabal, Irfan Hussain
Khalifa University, ARIC Center
arXiv Code Supplementary Video

Real-world marine datasets unified under one benchmark.

Abstract

We introduce FishDet-M, the first unified benchmark for underwater fish detection, integrating 13 heterogeneous datasets into a common COCO format. FishDet-M supports robust model evaluation across diverse aquatic conditions and includes CLIP-based semantic guidance for model selection. We benchmark 28 state-of-the-art models, demonstrating the consistent superiority of the YOLOv12 family, particularly YOLO12x, in accuracy and speed. The benchmark enables reproducibility and paves the way for real-time marine monitoring, aquaculture automation, and underwater conservation.
